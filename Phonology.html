Welcome to La Belle Tinker's Phonology Tool!

This page is currently in development, but currently has the following functions implemented:

  • Clicking on the "vowels" "pulmonics", and "non-pulmonics" tabs will pull up the appropriate section of the inventory.
  • Clicking on a phoneme will add it to the inventory, highlighting it.
  • Right-clicking on a phoneme will play a recording of the phoneme. If no recording is available the phoneme will be grey when hovered over instead of blue and right clicking will display a tool-tip indicating there is no recording.*
  • Clicking and dragging between two phonemes in the diphthong section of the vowels tab will produce an arrow indicating a diphthong. Right-clicking on a diphthong arrow will remove it.
  • Selecting a pre-existing inventory from the "Select Inventory" dropdown will select all phonemes in the selected dialect and deselect all others.
Future features include:
  • A tool for marking allophones and diacritics such as aspiration or labialization.
  • Tools for phonotactics.
  • The ability to print a simplified version of your phonology.
  • The ability to save your phoneme inventories in cookies or downloaded files.

* All recordings come from Wikimedia and can be accessed here for vowels, here for pulmonic consonants, and here for non-pulmonic consonants. I am working on compiling separate creative commons references for each audio clip used.
